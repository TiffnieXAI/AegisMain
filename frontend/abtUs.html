ABOUT OUR COMPANY

The name SAGE is a tribute to the Semi-Automatic Ground Environment, the Cold War era air defense system that pioneered modern computing. Just as the SAGE computers processed massive amounts of radar data to protect the skies, our SAGE stands as a Security Administrator Guarded Embassy for the digital frontier. We have repurposed that mission of national defense into a mission of community and economic protection. SAGE is more than just a company name, it is a pact between people who believe that innovation belongs to everyone. It is a team of students from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP) who were chosen for their grit.
